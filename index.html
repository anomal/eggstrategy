Egg Incubation Strategy Simulation

What is the best incubation strategy for a limited-resource player to maximize the number of 10km egg hatches?

In this simulation, eggs are replenished every 1 km walked. Eggs are generated randomly for each simulation run. Other parameters can be adjusted below.
